# Flipkart-customer-service-operation-data-analysis 
One of the top Indian e-commerce sites, Flipkart, is trying to figure out how customer service affects customer retention. Flipkart seeks to determine key performance indicators and underlying trends impacting customer satisfaction by applying data analysis to customer call data and making use of Excel's features. In order to inform strategies to improve overall customer experience and loyalty, this project aims to identify specific problems within customer service operations that may be causing the observed decline in retention rates.

Flipkart sells a variety of goods, such as electronics, clothing, home goods, and more. In order to keep its competitive edge and guarantee customer satisfaction, Flipkart, a significant player in the online retail market, works hard to deliver great customer service.

Flipkart has seen a drop in customer retention rates recently. The business is concentrating on examining its customer service operations in order to find any underlying issues that might be causing the decline. Maintaining clients, fostering loyalty, and improving the general shopping experience all depend on providing excellent customer service.

This project's main goal is to analyze customer call data and evaluate different facets of customer service performance in order to ascertain whether and how it affects customer retention. Finding particular problems in customer service operations that might be influencing customer satisfaction and retention rates is the aim.

Objective
This project's primary objective is to improve customer retention by gaining insights into Flipkart's customer service operations. In order to comprehend how customer service elements affect customer retention and pinpoint particular areas in need of improvement, you will examine the dataset that has been provided.
